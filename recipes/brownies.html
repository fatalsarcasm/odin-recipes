in greedy ants
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • optional: 4 fun size snickers cut up
steps
  1. melt butter in a saucepan, take off heat
  2. stir in eggs sugar and vanilla
  3. beat/whisk in cocoa, flour, baking soda, and salt
  4. spread evenly in greased and lightly floured pan (add in snickers bits now)
  5. bake 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees farenheit (449.8167 kelvin)
